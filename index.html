Projects

Design and Analysis of SAR-ADC for Low Power circuits

An 8-bit Asynchronous Successive Approximation Register (SAR) Analog to Digital Converter (ADC) designed to leverage state-of-the-art 18nm FinFET technology. This advanced asynchronous SAR system comprises several integral components, including a meticulously engineered internal clock generator, a high-performance bootstrapped sample and hold circuit, a robust and highly efficient dynamic comparator, a precision Digital-to-Analog Converter (DAC), and a sophisticated SAR logic. One of the standout features of our design is its remarkably low power consumption, which is in the microwatt (uW) range. These characteristics positions our ADC as a compelling solution for applications that demand a delicate equilibrium between power efficiency, moderate resolution, and swift operation.
